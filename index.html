Robotic Systems
in the Edge–Cloud Continuum (RISE) 2026

Workshop of the Euro-Par 2026 Conference

Call for Papers

The rapid advancements in robots (industrial, mobile, humanoid, exploration, etc), edge and cloud computing have paved the way for the development of sophisticated robotic systems that leverage these computing technologies.

The Robotic Systems in the Edge–Cloud Continuum (RISE) is dedicated in exploring how robotics systems can be designed, implemented, and optimized by utilizing the edge-cloud continuum. At the same time, the workshop focus on how the respective hyper-distributed applications’ workload and data should be processed and stored in devices, in various edge computing layers and in centralized cloud resources, building an edge-cloud continuum.

The workshop aims to foster collaboration between the robotics and parallel/distributed systems communities, focusing on architectures, algorithms, and systems enabling real-time robotic perception, decision making, and coordination across distributed computing infrastructures.

Topics of Interest

  • Data processing and storage for robotic systems
  • Distributed and collaborative robotics
  • Integration of IoT and sensors with robots
  • Edge-cloud data synchronization and management
  • AI and machine learning for robotics
  • Cybersecurity for edge-cloud robotic systems
  • Networking, communication, and low-latency protocols for robotics
  • Autonomous system control, automation, and planning
  • Digital twins and simulation for robotic systems
  • Prototypes and real-world deployments

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished papers that are not currently under review for other conferences or journals.

The papers should be formatted according to the LNCS guidelines. Papers should be between 10 and 12 pages. Accepted papers will appear in the official Euro-Par 2026 Workshop Proceedings.
